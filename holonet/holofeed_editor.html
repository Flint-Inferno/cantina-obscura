FATHIER RACER NAME POOL
Each race picks 6 names at random from this pool.
You need at least 6 names. More names = more variety between races.
These should feel like Star Wars creature/racing names.
SPEED HOLOCHESS PLAYER POOL
16 names are picked each tournament. The previous winner always carries over with a ★.
You need at least 17 names so there's always someone new to replace the champion.
Names should feel like alien Star Wars competitor names.
EXCHANGE LISTINGS
Each stock shows a live scrolling line graph on the Exchange tab.
Symbol: short all-caps ticker (e.g. KDY).   Base price: starting credit value.   Volatility: price swing per tick — 0.01 = stable, 0.05 = very jumpy.
TIMING & SPEED SETTINGS
